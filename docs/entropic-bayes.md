# Entropic Bayes Module

## Purpose

The Entropic Bayes module is used in the fermentation forecasting layer of the platform.

Its purpose is to estimate readiness under uncertainty and turn fermentation variability into a usable planning signal.

## Operational value

For fermented sausage products, this module helps estimate when a product is likely to be ready for packaging.

This improves:

- packaging timing
- line readiness
- supply chain synchronization
- decision quality under uncertainty

## Broader significance

Beyond operational value, this module reflects a research-driven approach to industrial decision systems by linking entropy-sensitive Bayesian reasoning with a real manufacturing problem.
