# System Overview

## Decision chain

The platform evaluates planning as a connected industrial decision problem rather than a single-step scheduling task.

It combines:

- production capacity
- packaging feasibility
- material and MRP constraints
- purchasing due dates
- available and inbound raw materials
- stock pressure
- what-if alternatives
- explainable decision logic

## Why this matters

In many real operations, the challenge is not generating a plan on paper.
The challenge is generating a plan that can survive downstream reality.

This platform is designed to reduce that gap.

## Decision philosophy

The objective is not only to create a theoretically correct plan, but a more executable one.
